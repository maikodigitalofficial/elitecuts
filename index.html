FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need an appointment?

Walk-ins are always welcome. We recommend booking ahead during weekends to secure your preferred time slot and avoid waiting.

How long does a cut take?

A signature haircut takes about 30 minutes. Skin fades and full grooming packages take 45–60 minutes depending on complexity.

What payments do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa, cash, and all major mobile money transfers. Card payments coming soon.

Any loyalty program?

Every 6th visit is 50% off for returning clients. Follow us on Instagram for weekly specials and updates.
